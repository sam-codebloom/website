Our Mission:

To democratize access to tech for underserved and underrepresented communities, empowering them through coding education, seamless transition support, and a vibrant, lifelong community that supports well-rounded, impactful tech leaders.



Programs Designed for Every Stage

Grades 3–5: Spark Curiosity

Engage with story games, unplugged logic challenges, and collaborative team robotics.

Grades 6–8: Build Foundations

Dive into coding puzzles, beginner robotics, and core algorithmic thinking.

Grades 9–12: Innovate & Create

Learn to build circuits, automate systems, and develop an engineer's mindset.

College: Lead & Connect

Participate in project-based, peer-led, and community-driven labs for advanced learning.
