Who We Are

At CodeBloom, we believe tech should be for everyone. Our mission is to democratize access to tech for underserved and underrepresented communities — through hands-on coding education, seamless career transitions, and a vibrant community built to last.

We’re here to break barriers, open doors, and make space for new voices in tech — not just for now, but for the long run.
